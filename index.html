The MicroPHP Manifesto

Not only is this site pretty broken, but it's out of date.

The manifesto was written in a time when it was hard to find high-quality components that weren't tied to heavy, "batteries-included" frameworks.

This has changed. Composer and packagist.org have done their job. Most heavy frameworks are made up of components now, and it's reasonable to pick and choose your tools at will.

PHP dev is in a much better place. Enjoy it. Use the tools you see fit.
